Step 1: Start the quiz.

Click on the first question to begin. Only one question will appear at a time, so focus on your choice before moving to the next one.

Step 2:

Each question has multiple choices. Pick the one that best reflects your feelings. There are no right or wrong answers!

Step 3:

After answering all the questions, your final result will appear. It will tell you whether your feelings lean more toward “LOVE” or “HATE” and provide a fun description.

Step 4: Understanding Your Results

  • You LOVE me! Your answers show that you secretly care deeply and are emotionally invested. Expect a playful, affectionate result description.
  • You HATE me! Your answers indicate distance or indifference. The result will be humorous but honest about your feelings.

Tips for Best Experience

  1. Take the quiz on a device in portrait mode for best layout.
  2. Answer all questions before closing the page to see accurate results.
  3. Enjoy the playful descriptions. this is all for fun!
