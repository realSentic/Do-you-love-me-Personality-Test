About SPT

(Sentic Personality Tests)

Hey! Welcome to SPT, which stands for Sentic Personality Tests. I made this website because I’ve always been interested in personality tests, and I also love coding. So I thought, why not combine the two? It’s a fun way for me to practice coding while sharing something entertaining and insightful with others.


SPT is all about taking simple, playful quizzes that give you results based on your choices. After you finish a test, you’ll see a little description that explains what your answers say about your personality. The goal isn’t to be serious or “right” or “wrong," it’s just a fun way to reflect on yourself a bit and enjoy the process.


Thanks for checking out SPT! Dive into a test, see what your results say about you, and most importantly… have fun.
